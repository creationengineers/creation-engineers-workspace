Line and Load Choke

CREATION ENGINEERS is one of the most renowned manufacturers of Line and Load Choke, also known as Input and Output Choke, used before and after Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) respectively. Our RL Line/Load Reactors are best-in-class power quality units with a long history of proven performance. Rugged and robust, they absorb power line disturbances that can damage or shut down VFDs and other sensitive equipment.

They work on both the line side and load side, providing an easy solution to reduce nuisance tripping, harmonic distortion, and long lead effects. Improved power quality, enhanced productivity, and complete peace of mind are achieved with RL Line/Load Reactors.

Applications

  • Motor drives and adjustable speed drive systems
  • Elevators
  • Robots
  • Machinery
  • Process automation equipment
  • Solar inverters
  • Wind converters
  • Traction converters

Features

  • Best-in-class line/load reactors.
  • Most robust, highest continuous service factor.
  • Low watts loss.
  • Unmatched performance and durability.
  • Reduction of inrush and peak currents.
  • Reliable and economical.
  • Provision of 0.5–7% impedance.
  • Designed to work smoothly under overload conditions.

Specifications

  • Type: Line and Load Reactor
  • Power capacity: Up to 600HP
  • Impedance: 0.5% to 7%
  • Voltage: Up to 660V system voltage
  • Current: Up to 1500Amp
  • Frequency: 50/60Hz
  • Insulation: Class F & Class H
  • Conductor: Copper or Aluminium
  • Core: CRGO/CRNGO
