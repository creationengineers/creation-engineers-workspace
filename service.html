Auto Transformer

Regulates voltage efficiently, ensuring stable power supply for industrial and commercial applications.

Control Transformer

Provides stable low-voltage power for control circuits, ensuring consistent operation and system safety.

3ph Isolation Transformer

Safely isolates electrical systems, reduces noise, and delivers dependable power to sensitive equipment.

3ph Control Transformer

Ensures stable voltage for industrial systems, improving reliability and operational efficiency.

Load Reactor

Reduces harmonic distortion and limits inrush current, ensuring reliable and efficient system performance.

Line Reactor (Type 1)

Protects against electrical noise and voltage spikes, ensuring system stability and longevity.

Line Reactor (Type 2)

Prevents power disturbances, ensuring smooth and uninterrupted system operation.

3ph Detuned Reactor

Minimizes harmonics and ensures efficient operation in power factor correction systems.

CT

Measures electrical current accurately, ensuring safe monitoring and control in power systems.

PT

A Potential Transformer (PT) steps down high voltages for accurate measurement and protection.
